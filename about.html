Who the heck
is this dude?

I'm a fresh graduate of the MSc Design program at Linköping University, specifically in the Visual Media track. I have a background in graphic and communication design, but over the years I’ve been working with different design disciplines, always with people and their needs at the center. I see my role as a designer as someone who can study a complicated phenomenon and make it accessible to the different people involved.

I consider myself a rational person and I constantly strive for a pragmatic and functional solution that adapts to everyone's needs, analyzing individual issues to develop a better general system.
