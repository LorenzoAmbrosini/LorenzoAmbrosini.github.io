Who the heck
is this dude?

I'm a fresh graduate in the MSc Design program at Linköping University, specifically in the Visual Media track. I have a background in graphic and communication design, but over the last year and a half my attention shifted to the world of data visualization.

I find that making a complex system of data visually understandable to a non-expert eye is an interesting challenge, especially in an era like ours where we are overloaded with information and it becomes essential to organize it and make it truthful and understandable for everyone.
